Upload the group to invite

Upload a spreadsheet listing the URNs of the schools you would like to invite listed vertically in column A..

The schools you list will all receive an invite from you on their invites list on the service. If anyone has registered the school they will receive an email notification about the invite. If no one has registered the school, then the invite will be waiting until somebody does register and view or accept the invite.

TBC! This service will give you a report indicating which invites have triggered notifications.

If you have previously invited any of these schools or if the URN is not recognised, the invite for this school will not be sent. This will be indicated on the report.

The file can be Excel or CSV
